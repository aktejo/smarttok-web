History

SmartTok remembers every card it's shown you, so the feed won't repeat itself. The History tab keeps your last 100. Clearing it lets old cards reappear.

About

SmartTok pulls directly from public APIs — no tracking, no ads. Most sources are called straight from your browser; the rest route through a small proxy (see README) since their APIs don't allow direct browser access. Each source is an interchangeable adapter, so new ones can be added without touching the feed or card code — everything available is listed under Sources above.
